# Tiramisu

Triamisu is Helen's favourite dessert, so it's often found in our fridge. It's also easy to whip up for parties!

I'm a visual guy, so here is a tiramisu flow chart. If you want more detailed instructions, continue reading below.

![A fairly complex diagram with 22 different boxes all connected with arrows, describing how to create a Tiramisu.](/images/recipes/tiramisu_diagram.png)

## Ingredient List

- 6 Espresso Shots
- Marsala (Fortified Wine) (I use "Boronia")
- Khalúa
- 300g Cream
- 250g Mascapone
- 1/3 Cups Sugar
- 3 Eggs
- 400g bag of Savoiardi Biscuits
- Icing Sugar (for dusting)
- Cocoa Powder (for dusting)

## Method

1. To create the coffee mixture, combine 6 espress shots, with 1/4 cups of Boronia, and 1/4 cups of Khalua. You can use only Khalua, or only Boronia, or even just coffee here. It doesn't matter too much, but I find that a mix of half-half is my favourite.

## Optional Story

Growing up, my Dad would make Tiramisu for special occasions. It was a rare and special dish because he always said it "was hard to make" and "is always eaten instantly". Now, that second part is definitely true, but it's certainly not hard to make. I wish I had tried to make it earlier and realised how easy it was, because I'd have made so many more!
